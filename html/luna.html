LUNA

Accompanied by her allied beast, Luna swiftly comes into battle. She fights using her glaives which can bounce to multiple targets. But not just that, she can call down the power of the moon as a lunar barrage crushes her enemies.

Showers random nearby enemies with strikes from Luna's current level of Lucent Beam. These beams do not stun their targets, and there is a maximum number of times that a single target can be struck. Also turns day into night for 10.0 seconds.
