TEMPLAR ASSASSIN

Be careful when fighting Templar Assassin; she will place a lot of slowing traps while hiding invisibly. Once she comes out to the open, she will shred enemies with piercing psionic blades while deflecting attacks with her refracting shield.

ABILITIES

PSIONIC TRAP

Templar Assassin places mystical traps that invisibly monitor enemy movement. When sprung at her command, they exert a slowing influence of 30% in the area. Traps charge up to slow 60% after 4.0 seconds. Deals bonus damage when fully charged.
