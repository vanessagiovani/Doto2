LYCAN

Whether Lycan is in his human or wolf form, he still devastates enemies with his sharp claws and jaw. His wolf transformation grants him incredible speed and strength. Other than that, he can summon stealthy wolves to scout and fight alongside him.

ABILITIES

SHAPESHIFT

Lycan assumes his lupine form, granting him and his units critical strikes and added vision at night. During Shapeshift, Lycan and all units under his control move at increased speed, cannot be slowed and have a 40% chance to deal a critical strike doing 150% damage.
