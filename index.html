Gameplay

HEROES

Hero roles are divided into core and support.

While the core's damage carries their team to

victory, the support assists the core by healing

and other buffs.

Heroes have levels between 1 and 30 where they become more powerful as

they're leveling up. On level 6, heroes can use their ultimate. All heroes have

strength, intelligence, and agility as their attributes where it affects their

health points, mana points, and attack speed, respectively.
